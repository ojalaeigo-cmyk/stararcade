Privacy Policy for Star King

Last updated: 7 June 2026

Overview

Star King is a casual arcade game. The app does not require an account and does not collect personal data on a server.

Data collection

Star King does not collect, transmit, sell or share personal data with third parties.

Local leaderboard

If the player enters a name for the local leaderboard, that name and score are stored only on the player’s own device/browser local storage. This data is not sent to a server.

Permissions

The app does not use location, camera, microphone, contacts, calendar, SMS, phone, files or payment permissions.

Advertising and purchases

Star King does not include advertising and does not include in-app purchases.

Children

Star King is not specifically designed for children under 13. The intended audience is 13+.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the support email listed on the Google Play Store page.
